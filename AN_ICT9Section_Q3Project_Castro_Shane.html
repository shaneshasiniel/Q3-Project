TIPS TO PREVENT FIRE HAPPENING IN HOME

1. Never Leave The Kitchen Unattended When Cooking
2. Store any Flammable Materials on a Safe Place
3. Check Your Heating Sources
4. Manage the Wires
5. Be Cautious in Open Flames
6. Keep a Fire Extinguisher at All Times
7. Unplug Items When Not In Use
